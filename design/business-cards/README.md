# JTLH LTD — business cards (85 × 55 mm)

Print-ready SVG fronts for a UK standard card. **Square (sharp) corners.** QR is square; card is rectangular. QR encodes `https://jtlhltd.com`.

## Specs

- Size: **85 × 55 mm**
- Corners: **square / sharp** (not rounded)
- Colours: black `#050505`, gold `#E8C36A`, muted `#A3A3A3`
- Rebuild: `node design/business-cards/build-cards.js` (needs `qrcode` available locally)

## Designs

| File | Idea |
|------|------|
| `01-qr-dominant.svg` | Brand left, large gold QR right |
| `02-split-panel.svg` | Brand + contact left, QR panel right |
| `03-minimal-scan.svg` | Large QR left, thin brand right |
| `04-contact-strip.svg` | QR right + gold contact bar |
| `05-gold-field.svg` | Brand left, gold end with dark QR |
| `00-reverse-brand.svg` | Optional reverse: mark + JTLH LTD |

## Recommendation

**01** or **03** front + **00** reverse on soft-touch black, sharp corners.
